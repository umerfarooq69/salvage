What is a Certificate of Destruction (CoD)?

A Certificate of Destruction (CoD) proves that you have disposed of your car or vehicle correctly. This prevents you from having any further liability for the vehicle and any potential penalisations that could come with it. These are an official DVLA document that is issued by a scrapyard or breaker's yard when they recycle your scrap car.
